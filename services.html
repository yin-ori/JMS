Turn Your Thesis Into a Project

Successfully Complete Your Thesis On Time

These workshops guide university students toward academic success by introducing structured, project-based approaches to thesis planning.

Why It Matters

Writing a thesis is a time-intensive and complex challenge — especially for students navigating academic systems in a foreign country. From adapting to unfamiliar expectations to managing cultural and linguistic hurdles, international students often need more than academic know-how to succeed.

My workshops provide clarity and structure, helping students approach their thesis with confidence and purpose.

What I Offer

  • Flexible Delivery: Half-day workshops online or on-site
  • Follow-up Support: Optional one-on-one sessions for personal feedback
  • Language Options: Primarily in English, available in Japanese upon request
  • Customizable: Tailored formats to fit your institution and students’ needs

Why Me?

  • Academic Insight: Studied in Germany, Japan, and Norway — including Bonn University, Tohoku University, and UiT Arctic University of Norway
  • Project Expertise: Certified Agile Product Owner and PMI CAPM with years of experience in cross-cultural IT project management
  • Student-Focused: I understand the pressures students face — and help break down thesis writing into achievable milestones

Key Benefits for Students

  • Structured Guidance: Practical strategies and tools to plan and complete their thesis
  • Cross-Cultural Awareness: Support that understands and respects cultural differences in academic expectations
  • Individual Support: Optional one-on-one sessions for personalized guidance
